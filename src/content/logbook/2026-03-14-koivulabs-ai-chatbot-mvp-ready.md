## KoivuLabs AI Chatbot MVP Ready

Today marks an important milestone: KoivuLabs' first AI chatbot product is now in production.

### What We Built

KoivuLabs AI Chatbot is a white-label solution designed for Finnish small and medium-sized enterprises. It provides a custom AI assistant for their websites. The chatbot learns from the company's own documents—brochures, guides, product information—and answers customer questions based on this data.

Crucially, the bot doesn't invent answers. It only responds with what it has been taught or honestly admits if it lacks the information.

### The Technology

We constructed the product using a modern, scalable stack:

- **RAG Architecture**: Documents are stored in a vector database from which the bot retrieves relevant information before each response.
- **GPT-4o-mini**: A fast and cost-effective language model.
- **Fully Brandable**: Customizable with the client's colors, name, and greeting message.
- **One-Line Embedding**: Works on any website with a single line of code.

### Why This Matters

Businesses frequently receive the same questions about opening hours, prices, and processes. The AI chatbot answers these 24/7 without staffing costs, providing precise answers based on the specific company's data.
