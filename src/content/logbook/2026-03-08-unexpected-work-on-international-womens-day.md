Today was supposed to be a day off. I had planned to relax, play some games, and take a long nap before my evening shift. But, as it turns out, Lovable had a special offer for International Women's Day: free, unlimited access to their tool. So, guess what? My entire day has been spent working with it.

I used the opportunity to start new projects. Currently, I'm developing an MVP for a project called Kuntapulssi. It's intended to be an interface between residents and their local government. The goal is to involve citizens in decision-making and provide a feedback channel for everyone. The hope is that this will benefit the community.

We'll see how it goes. It's a bit of a detour from my planned day, but sometimes these unexpected turns lead to valuable progress. I'm learning as I go, and that's part of the journey. The tool has been helpful, and I'm curious to see how Kuntapulssi will develop in the coming weeks.
