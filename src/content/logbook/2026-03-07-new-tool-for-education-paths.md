Today, I embarked on a new project focused on education. The idea came from the need to find a new direction or additional qualifications, especially when formal credentials are lacking, as in my case.

I started researching what educational opportunities are available, what courses exist, and how they can contribute to career goals. This led me to develop a tool aimed at addressing these needs. The tool helps users explore different educational paths and find what suits them best.

I'm now looking for testers. If you're interested in exploring new educational directions or need assistance in finding suitable courses, your feedback would be invaluable. This is a small step, but it could be a practical aid for many in similar situations.
