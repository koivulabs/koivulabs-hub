### Reviving Maso Hockey Manager

Does anyone remember Maso Hockey Manager (MHM)? It was a quirky ice hockey simulator from the late 90s and early 2000s, filled with humor and Finnish hockey legends. Yesterday, it popped into my mind when I came across a tweet about someone making a football manager game for browsers. It clicked for me—why not bring MHM back for today’s audience?

I’ve started working on a prototype. It’s already online, though it still needs some polishing and tweaking. The goal is to capture the essence of the original while updating it for modern platforms. This process has been a learning experience, reminding me that small, concrete steps are essential.

I hope to have the game ready for everyone to play soon. Once it's polished, I'll share the link with all who are interested. Stay tuned for updates as I continue to refine this nostalgic project, blending past charm with current technology.
