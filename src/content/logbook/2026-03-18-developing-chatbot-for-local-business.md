## Inspiration from Unexpected Places

Yesterday's meeting in Jyväskylä sparked some ideas, despite the initial reception being lukewarm. Often in project work, people are protective of their positions and hesitant to embrace technical solutions. This can lead to projects that focus on superficial tasks rather than meaningful change.

## Turning Ideas into Action

However, the meeting inspired me to develop a chatbot idea that could support the project's goals. The aim is to translate friendliness towards pets into concrete business benefits for entrepreneurs. This has led me to refine my strategy and develop a product that I am excited to present to Harri Uusisalo, who oversees business services in Saarijärvi.

## A Tool for Engagement and Visibility

The tool I've designed will assist various organizations and municipalities by involving locals in decision-making and providing data to leaders. It will also enhance the visibility of local businesses using SEO and AI tools. I am hopeful that this approach will be well-received and that the benefits will be evident to all involved. Today holds another interesting meeting, but first, it's time to train my puppy, Max, who is eagerly waiting.
