Today was quite a day. Lovable's offer to use their tool for free had me glued to my screen. Once I realized I could open multiple projects in Lovable and set them all to work simultaneously, it felt like a revelation.

The amount of work and the number of projects I have in progress is overwhelming. I need to compile and present what I've accomplished so far. It's not often that inspiration strikes this intensely, but I'm hesitant to market or advertise anything.

Finding a solution to this marketing challenge is crucial. I need to balance the excitement of new ideas with the practicality of finishing and promoting them. AI tools are helpful collaborators, but they don't replace the need for clear strategy and planning.
