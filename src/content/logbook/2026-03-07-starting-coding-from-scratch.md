## Beginning the Coding Journey

In my forties, I decided to embark on a new path: coding. This wasn't a decision made lightly. Coming from a non-technical background, I knew the learning curve would be steep. However, with a pragmatic approach and a rural Finnish work ethic, I was ready to tackle this challenge head-on.

## Embracing the Learning Process

Studying at JAMK University of Applied Sciences while building real products has been a rewarding experience. I am constantly figuring out new concepts, and I embrace the process of learning openly. Each small, concrete step I take matters more than any grand vision. It's about steady progress and real work, not about hype or shortcuts.

## The Role of AI in My Journey

AI is a fascinating tool and collaborator. It's not magic, and I never hype it. Instead, I focus on how it can assist me in my projects. Authentic struggle is part of my story, and I acknowledge when things don't work out as planned. This honest reflection helps me grow and improve, ensuring that my journey into coding is grounded in reality.
