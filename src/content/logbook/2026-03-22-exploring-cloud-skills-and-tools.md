I've spent the past few days testing various cloud skills and ready-made repositories. This exploration included setting up a couple of local language model systems, running either Crew AI or my custom agent setup. I'll share more about my setup later.

What stands out is the sheer volume of information available. Many resources today are just copies of copies. Social media channels often push content designed to capture attention, but it quickly becomes clear that much of it is just engagement bait. This raises an important question: are any of these tools genuinely useful, or are the developers' internal interfaces and established methods the best options?

This is a topic worth exploring further. I suspect many others are in a similar situation, grappling with the same issues. It's crucial to discern which tools add real value and which are simply noise in the ever-growing digital landscape.
